# Learning Program Flyer Prompts (Gemini)

Prompts for generating elegant promotional flyers for the Insureversia Learning Program: "AI in Insurance: The Learning Program."

**Brand reference:**
- Logo: `https://insureversia.com/assets/logos/insureversia-logo-horizontal-white.png`
- Logomark only: `https://insureversia.com/assets/logos/insureversia-just-logo-white.png`
- Colors: Navy `#0F2B46`, Gold `#C9A84C`, Teal `#00B4D8`
- Fonts: DM Serif Display (headings), Inter (body)

---

## 1. Vertical Flyer (Portrait — 1080 x 1920 px)

```
Design an elegant, modern vertical flyer (1080 x 1920 px, portrait orientation) for a professional learning program called "AI in Insurance: The Learning Program" by Insureversia.

LAYOUT (top to bottom):
- TOP BAND: Deep navy (#0F2B46) background occupying the top 35% of the flyer. Place the Insureversia logo (use this image: https://insureversia.com/assets/logos/insureversia-logo-horizontal-white.png) centered near the top with comfortable padding. Below the logo, place the program title "AI in Insurance" in large, elegant serif typography (white), and directly below it "The Learning Program" in a slightly smaller weight. Below the title, add a single gold (#C9A84C) horizontal rule or thin decorative line.

- MIDDLE SECTION: Clean white or very light warm gray background. Display three key pillars as a vertical stack of elegant cards or blocks, each with a small gold icon on the left and text on the right:
  1. "Hyper-Personalized" — "Tailored to your role, goals, and learning style"
  2. "Hands-On Practical" — "Case studies, simulations, real-world scenarios"
  3. "Smoother Methodology" — "Active learning across nine progressive levels"

- LOWER SECTION: A subtle teal (#00B4D8) accent stripe or gradient transition. Display the program formats in a compact, elegant way:
  "18 formats · 5 categories · 1h to multi-week"
  Below that: "Self-Learning · Coached · In-Person"

- BOTTOM BAND: Navy background again. Centered text:
  "Request Your Program"
  "insureversia.com/learn/program"
  Place the small Insureversia logomark (use this image: https://insureversia.com/assets/logos/insureversia-just-logo-white.png) and the tagline "Insurance. Reinvented. Verified. Supercharged with AI." in small italic gold text.

STYLE:
- Sophisticated, corporate-elegant, not startup-casual
- Generous whitespace, clean typographic hierarchy
- Color palette strictly: navy #0F2B46, gold #C9A84C, teal #00B4D8, white, light gray
- No stock photography — rely on typography, color blocks, and subtle geometric accents
- Suitable for LinkedIn stories, Instagram stories, or print at A4/Letter size
```

## 5. Tracks-Focused Vertical Flyer (Portrait — 1080 x 1920 px)

```
Design an elegant, modern vertical flyer (1080 x 1920 px, portrait orientation) highlighting the three specialized learning tracks of a professional program called "AI in Insurance: The Learning Program" by Insureversia.

LAYOUT (top to bottom):

- TOP BAND (~18%): Deep navy (#0F2B46) background. Insureversia logo (use this image: https://insureversia.com/assets/logos/insureversia-logo-horizontal-white.png) centered near the top. Below it: "Find Your Track" in large, elegant serif typography (white). Below that: "Choose the path designed for your role" in smaller white text. Thin gold (#C9A84C) decorative line.

- THREE TRACKS (~65%): White or very light warm gray background. Three large, distinct track cards stacked vertically with generous spacing. Each card has:
  - A colored left border (gold for Executive, teal for Technical, green for Frontline)
  - A role icon or geometric shape at the top
  - Track name in bold navy serif (e.g., "Executive Track")
  - "For:" line in small uppercase gray (e.g., "C-suite, VPs, Directors")
  - Duration badge on the right side (e.g., "8–12 hours")
  - Module list in compact body text:

  CARD 1 — EXECUTIVE TRACK (gold #C9A84C accent):
    For: C-suite, VPs, Directors
    Duration: 8–12 hours
    "AI Foundations · AI Applications · Ethics & Responsible AI · Building Your AI Roadmap + Executive briefings"

  CARD 2 — TECHNICAL TRACK (teal #00B4D8 accent):
    For: Actuaries, Data teams, IT
    Duration: 16–24 hours
    "AI Foundations · AI Readiness Profile · Prompt Engineering · Underwriting in the AI Era · Data Strategy + Technical deep-dives"

  CARD 3 — FRONTLINE TRACK (green accent, use a complementary green like #2ECC71 or the brand teal):
    For: Underwriters, Claims adjusters, Brokers
    Duration: 12–16 hours
    "AI Foundations · AI Readiness Profile · Prompt Engineering · AI Applications · Claims or Customer Experience + Role simulations"

- BOTTOM BAND (~17%): Navy background.
  - "All tracks include hands-on tools:" in small white text

  - "Request Your Program" in gold
  - "insureversia.com/learn/program/curriculum" in white
  - Small Insureversia logomark (use this image: https://insureversia.com/assets/logos/insureversia-just-logo-white.png)

STYLE:
- Sophisticated, corporate-elegant with clear visual hierarchy per track
- Each track card should feel distinct via its color accent but unified in layout
- Generous whitespace, clean typographic hierarchy
- Color palette strictly: navy #0F2B46, gold #C9A84C, teal #00B4D8, white, light gray (plus green accent for Frontline)
- No stock photography — rely on typography, color accents, and subtle geometric elements
```
