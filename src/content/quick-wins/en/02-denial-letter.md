## What This Quick Win Does

Drafting claims denial letters is a routine but critical task. Each letter must be clear, compliant with regulations, properly cite policy language, and maintain a professional tone. AI can generate a well-structured draft in minutes, letting you focus on accuracy and compliance review.

## How to Use It

### Step 1: Gather Your Information

Before using the prompt, compile:
- Claim and policy numbers
- Insured's information
- Date and description of loss
- Relevant policy provisions (copy the exact language)
- Investigation findings supporting the denial
- Applicable state regulatory requirements

### Step 2: Generate the Draft

Paste the prompt and your claim information into your AI tool. Review the output carefully.

### Step 3: Verify and Customize

- Cross-check every policy citation against the actual policy
- Verify regulatory disclosure requirements for your jurisdiction
- Adjust tone and language as appropriate
- Have the letter reviewed before sending

## What This Does Not Replace

This does not replace claims adjuster judgment, legal review, or regulatory compliance expertise.
